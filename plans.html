Implementation Plans

The implementation of a Software Metrics Programme implies an important change in the way of working and thinking in people. Success will only be reached if the implementation is dealt with as if it were a project, with the corresponding plans, budgets, resource assignments, process controls, revisions and most definitely the commitment from management of TravelWare. The project will follow a life cycle. The measurement process to be implemented will have to be adaptable and manageable.

Any life cycle model requires:

  • A previous stage of adaptation to TravelWare's environment.
  • It will be sufficiently flexible as to change according to its needs
  • It has to be planned and the activities planned will be communicated to all employees.
Software metrics should be taken on little by little, fixing objectives, which once reached will give way to the establishment of new goals.

Steps involved in developing a Successful Metrics Strategy

  1. Identify the Metrics Customers within TravelWare

    • Functional Management
    • Project Management
    • Software Developers/Engineers
    • Test Managers/Testers
    • Others - SQA, Technical Support, Auditors, Help Desk, Marketing
    • Clients/Users

  2. Identify and define the Business goals

  3. Sketch key metrics to support the vision, mission, and goals

  4. Gather historical data

  5. Automate measurement procedures

  6. Use metrics in the management structure

  7. Define Reporting Mechanisms

  8. Train All Employees on how to gather the data
